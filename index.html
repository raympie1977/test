Our Projects

We don't have customers. We team up and join you on your dream to deliver the best. We work for real estate developers and investors, leisure operators, and gouvernments. Our work is always customized, based on the needs of the project. Together with you, we decide what steps to take. We are focussing on planning, quality and budget to really get things done.

Some inspiring examples of the projects we work(ed) on:

Expansion

We have performed a market analysis for the expansion of Koezio to the Netherlands, including a site selection and the introduction with the developers.

Expansion

Lot66 has asked us to realize the expansion of the Lot66 Family Entertainment Center Concept to 10 sites in Europe.

Vaals

Redevelopment of the historic City Hall of Vaals (Netherlands), into a flabbergasting, profitable cultural heritage attraction.
